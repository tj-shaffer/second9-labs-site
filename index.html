How we work

Four steps. No theatre.

We're allergic to long discovery phases and eight-figure consulting decks. Here's the actual rhythm.

STEP 01

Listen

A free 30-minute call. You tell us what's painful. We ask the kind of questions that come from having seen this movie before.

STEP 02

Diagnose

A short paid diagnostic — usually a week or two. We map what you've got, what's broken, and the smallest fix with the biggest upside.

STEP 03

Build

We ship something useful within a few weeks. We show our work, bring you along, and make sure nothing is a black box.

STEP 04

Hand off

You own what we built. Clear documentation, training for your team, and a small optional retainer if you want us on speed dial.

The incredible tools at our fingertips make it a wonderful time to solve the small, annoying problems that quietly eat people's days. That's the work worth doing.

Honest answers

Questions you're probably about to ask.

We're not a tech company — will you still work with us?

Honestly, those are our favourite clients. If you're a firm, a shop, a studio, a clinic, a nonprofit — anywhere where software is a tool rather than the product — we speak your language and we won't bury you in jargon.

How much does this cost?

Diagnostics start around the cost of a nice weekend away. Build engagements are scoped after the diagnostic, so you know the number before you commit. We'll never surprise you with a bill.

What's "vibe coding," and should I be worried?

It's the fast, AI-assisted style of building software that's become possible in the last couple of years. We use it to get prototypes into your hands in days rather than months. Everything that ships to production is still reviewed, tested, and documented by a human — us.

Will we own the code and tools you build?

Yes, fully. No lock-in. We hand over the repository, the documentation, and enough training that your team could hire someone else to take over if you ever wanted to.

What about our data and privacy?

Taken seriously. We prefer tools and architectures that keep your data in your control, we're clear about what any AI component does and doesn't see, and we put it in writing.

Where are you based?

Second 9 Labs is a small LLC based in the United States. We work remotely with clients anywhere in North America, and we're happy to meet in person when it helps.
